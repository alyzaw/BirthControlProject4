Welcome to the wide world of


Birth Control

We know it can be overwhelming to find a method that works for you, so we’re here to help.


Using birth control can come with unwelcome side effects. The pamphlets in your OB/GYN’s office don’t always paint a clear picture of how it might affect your life. That’s why we’ve parsed through thousands of user reviews to learn about the real experiences of using different birth control methods.

Check Out This App

Do you Have Trouble Keeping up with your Birth Control Method? Try The Spot On Tracker App.

Spot On is a period and birth control tracking mobile app

available for Android and IOS phones that can help

you stay on top of your birth control method and track your cycle. The App provides customized appointment reminders, and puts birth control and sexual health resources from the experts at Planned Parenthood. For more information visit Planned Parenthood's page
